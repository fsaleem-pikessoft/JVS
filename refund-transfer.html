How It Works

Simple, secure, and convenient refund transfer process

1

Ask for a Refund Transfer

Tell your tax professional that you want to pay for tax preparation with your refund. No upfront payment required.

2

Select Your Disbursement

Choose how you want to receive your refund and Fast Cash Advance (if applicable) from our convenient options.

3

File Your Tax Return

When your tax return is filed, it will include instructions to send your refund to Santa Barbara Tax Products Group (SBTPG).

4

Receive Your Refund

Once we receive your refund, all requested fees are taken from your refund and the rest is paid to you through your chosen method.
