State Refund FAQs

Clear answers for common questions

Confirm you’re using the exact refund amount from your filed return, your correct filing status, and the primary filer’s SSN. If you recently filed, allow processing time per your state’s guidance.

For e‑filed returns, most states recommend waiting at least 2–3 weeks; for paper returns, 8–12+ weeks. Use the phone icon next to your state for official assistance if it’s past these windows.

States may offset refunds for past‑due obligations (child support, taxes, student loans). Your state notice will explain any offsets and next steps.
