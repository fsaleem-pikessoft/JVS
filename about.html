About JVS Tax Services

My Mantra is SIMPLICITY

In 2001, my journey in tax preparation began after my successful completion of the tax preparation course at H&R Block. I was then hired by them as a tax preparer. During my tenure with H&R Block, I've completed numerous courses to increase my knowledge of the tax codes and regulations both at the Federal & State level. I have since been preparing individual, business, and foreign tax returns for a diverse body of clients nationwide.

With 24 years experience in tax preparation, I've decided to start a new and exciting chapter by launching JVS Tax Services, to use the knowledge I've acquired over the years to bring ease and peace of mind to my clients during the tax preparation process. I will use my acquired skills to identify potential tax credits and liabilities to consistently deliver accurate and timely tax preparation services.

When you contact JVS Tax Services, we'll respond within 24 hours. I personally speak with everyone and tailor approaches to each individual.
