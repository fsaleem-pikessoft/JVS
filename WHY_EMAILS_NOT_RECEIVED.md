## üîç Troubleshooting FormSubmit

### If verification email not received:

1. **Check Spam Folder** - This is where 90% of verification emails go
2. **Wait 10 minutes** - Sometimes delayed
3. **Submit form again** - Triggers new verification email
4. **Check email filters** - Make sure nothing is blocking FormSubmit
5. **Try different browser** - Sometimes helps

### After verification, if emails still not arriving:

1. Check spam folder for form submissions
2. Make sure verification was completed
3. Try submitting form again
4. Check FormSubmit status at: https://formsubmit.co/status
